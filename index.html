Dr. rer. nat. Pawan Goyal

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Max Planck Institute (MPI-DCTS), Magdeburg, Germany in the group of Prof. Dr. Peter Benner. In 2018, I received my Ph.D. degree in the field of applied mathematics in his group as well.

Broadly speaking, I am a numerical analyst and data scientist. My main research interests include data-driven identification and reduced-order modeling of dynamical systems, image reconstruction, and compressive sensing [more detail].
I am currently a part of Max Planck research network on big data-driven material science (BiGmax) as well.
